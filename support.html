Common questions

My render failed. Check your connection and try again — failed renders never consume a credit.

I bought Premium but it's not active. Open Profile → Restore purchases. If it still doesn't unlock, email us with your purchase receipt.

How do I cancel my subscription? Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Casavia.

How do I delete my account? Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, designs, and usage data.
