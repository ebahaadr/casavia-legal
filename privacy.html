Casavia — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

Casavia ("the app", "we") is an interior-design app that redesigns photos of your rooms using AI. This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

Data we collect

  • Account information. You can use Casavia without creating an account (an anonymous identifier is created for you). If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and name as provided by that service, and store them with your account.
  • Photos you upload. Room photos you choose are uploaded to our rendering service to generate redesigned versions. Photos are used only to produce your designs — never for advertising, and never to train AI models.
  • Your designs and usage counters. Generated designs, the rooms and styles you pick, and your free-render usage are stored with your account so your history syncs across devices and reinstalls.
  • Purchase status. Subscriptions and credit packs are processed by Apple. Our payments partner (RevenueCat) tells us whether your account has an active purchase. We never see your payment details.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not show ads or share data with advertisers.
  • We do not use your photos for anything except generating your designs.

Services we rely on

  • Firebase (Google) — sign-in and cloud storage of your account, designs, and usage counters.
  • Google Cloud — hosts the rendering service that processes your photos.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management.
  • Apple — payment processing and, if you choose, Sign in with Apple.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. You can delete your account at any time inside the app (Profile → Delete account), which permanently removes your account, designs, and usage data from our systems. You can also email us at the address below to request deletion.

Children

Casavia is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.
